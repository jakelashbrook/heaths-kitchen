WHY US?
We're a Dog Friendly Environment and aim to make your furbabies feel just as welcome and loved as our hooman customers! Feel free to request an order for your pooch whilst making your own!
We cater to all dietary needs at Heaths Kitchen, from Vegan to Vegatarian, Gluten Free options and removal off any ingredients that you may be allergic to where possible. We have a large menu with plenty alternatives should there be any issue or concern over what an item may contain.
We offer great value for money! We strive to offer you amazing ingredients from cuisines all around the UK and the world. Our Menu is inspired by our travels, and the chefs always prepare your food with the most important ingredient- LOVE.
Our team are famous for being friendly and helpful within the commmunity, it's why so many regulars just keeping coming back! If you're feeling sceptical, why not pop in for yourself and sample our cosy and warm environment? We're sure you wont be dissapointed!
THE FOOD

At Heaths Kitchen we have a menu full of variety for you to choose whatever best suits your taste buds or dietary needs. Whether you want Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten Free or a dish packed full of protein- we have something here waiting just for you!
